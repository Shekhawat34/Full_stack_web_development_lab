üéµ Audio Embedding

Basic Audio Player:

Basic Audio Code:

<audio controls> <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"> <source src="audio.ogg" type="audio/ogg"> Your browser does not support the audio element. </audio>

Audio with Preload:

<audio controls preload="metadata"> <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"> </audio>

Audio with Loop:

<audio controls loop> <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"> </audio>

Audio Attributes:

  • controls - Shows audio controls
  • autoplay - Starts automatically
  • loop - Repeats the audio
  • muted - Starts muted
  • preload - How much to preload

Preload Options:

  • none - Don't preload
  • metadata - Preload metadata only
  • auto - Preload entire file
